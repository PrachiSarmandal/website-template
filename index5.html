About Us

ARM Architects and Planners is working with a vision to provide Innovative, Affordable and Creative Solutions. Our focus is to provide our client with professional and timely service. We take every project as unique and different opportunity.

A successful architectural design is best achieved through effective partnership of all project stakeholders. We are a multi-disciplinary team of architects, planners, engineers, renewable energy experts and thinkers who constantly work on improving and providing best services to our clients. We start from conceptualize to project delivery under common roof.
